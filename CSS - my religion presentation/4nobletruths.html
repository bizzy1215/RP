The Four Noble Truths

Buddhist believe there are four gospels of the world, they are called the Four Noble Truths.


Dukkha: To live life is suffering.


Samudaya: There is a reason to this suffering, this reason is igornace and selfish desires.


Nirodha: When the ignorance and desire is reduced, so is the suffering.


Magga: The end of suffering is contained in enlightenment.
