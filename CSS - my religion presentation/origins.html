Origins Of Buddhism

Buddhism was founded around the 5th century BCE by prince Siddhartha Gautama in present day Nepal.


Siddhartha was born into a wealthy family but gave it up to explore the world.


He traveled and was moved by suffering of the world and decided to find spiritual anwsers throught meitation.


He meditated under a Bodhi tree for 6 years until he became enlightened


He became the Buddha, which means the enlightened one.


He had many followers and they were the ones who organized his beliefs into a religion upon his death in 400 BCE
