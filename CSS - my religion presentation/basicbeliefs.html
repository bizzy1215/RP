Basic beliefs of Buddhism

Some scholars do not recognize Buddhism as an organized religion, but rather, a "way of life"


Buddhism denies a supreme deity, the founder, the Buddha, is considered a an extraodrinary being, but not a god.


The goal of Buddhism is to become enlightened and reach nirvana. Nirvana is a state of mind gained when you eliminate greed, hatred, and ignorance within yourself.


You reach nirvana through meditation, good behavior, and physical labor.


Buddhist believe is karma and reincarnation
