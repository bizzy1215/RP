Symbols of Buddhism

There is no single Buddhist symbol, but a number of symbols

It was under a Bodhi tree where the Buddha became enlighted.


The Bodhi tree represents enlightenment.


The Dharma Wheel is a wheel that represents the eightfold path, which are eight practices:

  • right view
  • right intention
  • right speech
  • right action
  • right livelihood
  • right effort
  • right mindfulness
  • right concentration

The white lotus represents a state of mental purity(enlightenment).

The red lotus represents love and compassion.

The blue lotus represents wisdom and intelligence.

The gold lotus represents the achievement of enlightenment.


The ensō is a sacred symbol in Zen Buddhism meaning circle of togetherness.

It represents strength, unity, the life cycle, and beauty.

It is drawn using one brush stroke and is a form of meditation.

Zen Buddhism is a form of buddhism that emphasizes simplicity.
